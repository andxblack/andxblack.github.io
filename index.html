Andrew Black

I am a Lecturer in Applied Mathematics in the School of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Adelaide. Before this, I held an ARC Discovery Early Career Research Award (DECRA) Fellowship.

My research interests are in stochastic modelling, mathematical epidemiology, evolution, probabilistic learning and Bayesian inference. More details can be found in the Research section.

I am currently an Associate Investigator in the ARC Centre of Excellence for Mathematical and Statistical Frontiers ACEMS.

Open source code for some of my work can be found on EpiStruct and GitHub.
